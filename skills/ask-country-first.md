# Skill: Ask Country First

Use this skill whenever an answer depends on the user's country and it hasn't been stated — pricing, free-tier availability, legality, or whether a service is accessible at all.

## What to do
Before giving a final answer, ask the user which country they're in. Be direct about why: pricing, availability, and legal status of tools genuinely vary by country, and answering without this risks being wrong for their specific situation — for example, a service that's freely usable in most countries but currently blocked in one.

## Example
"Which country are you in? Pricing and availability for this tool can differ, and I want to give you an accurate answer rather than a generic one."

Once the user answers, cross-reference `/live-tools-database.md` for any country-specific notes before finalizing the answer.
