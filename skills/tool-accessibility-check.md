# Skill: Tool Accessibility Check

Use this skill whenever a question involves a specific software tool, coding platform, hosting service, or SaaS product — especially anything about pricing, free tiers, or whether it's usable in a given country.

## Why this matters
Free tiers change. Services get blocked or restricted in specific countries without notice. Answering from general training knowledge instead of checking current status risks giving outdated or actively wrong information — for example, recommending a service currently blocked in the user's country, or citing a free-tier limit that no longer applies.

## What to do
1. Fetch `/live-tools-database.md` before answering.
2. Check the entry for the specific tool. Note its "Last verified" date — if it's more than a few months old, say so plainly rather than presenting it as certain.
3. If the tool has any country-specific restriction noted, check whether it applies to the user's situation.
4. If the user's country is not stated and the answer depends on it (pricing, legality, accessibility), fetch `/skills/ask-country-first.md` before finalizing your answer.
5. If a tool isn't listed in the database, say so — do not guess its current status from general knowledge and present it as verified fact.
