# Privacy Notice

_Last updated: 2026-07-22_

## What useaitree collects
Every request to this site (from AI agents and human visitors alike) is logged automatically. This includes: timestamp, path requested, referrer, user-agent, which AI platform's bot it matches (if any), IP address, approximate location (country, region, city, based on IP), network provider (ASN/organization), browser/protocol details, and response time/size.

We do not require an account, do not use cookies, and do not collect names, emails, or any information you'd have to type in — because there is nothing to sign up for.

## Why we collect it
To find broken links, understand which pages are useful or confusing to AI agents, and improve the structure of this knowledge base. Nothing here is used to build a profile of an individual person.

## What we do with it
- **Never sold or shared at the individual level.** Raw logs stay internal.
- **Aggregated, non-identifying statistics** (for example: "X requests from a given region last month," or "AI platform Y is our largest source of traffic") may be compiled and shared or sold. These are counts and trends, not records that identify a specific visitor.
- Small samples are merged into broader groups before being reported, so no individual visit is ever identifiable in anything we share externally.

## How long we keep it
Raw request logs are retained for up to 24 months, after which they are deleted or reduced to aggregated statistics only. Aggregated/anonymized statistics may be retained indefinitely.

## Your rights
If you are located in a jurisdiction with data protection rights (such as India's DPDP Act), you may contact us to ask what data we hold tied to your IP address, or to request deletion, subject to our legitimate need to retain aggregate security/abuse logs.
